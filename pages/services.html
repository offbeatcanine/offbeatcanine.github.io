Audition

Following a free phone consultation, we'll schedule a visit to your home to observe your dog's behavior in their natural environment. During this session, we'll conduct a thorough behavioral evaluation and provide you with detailed insight on the behavior patterns. This visit gives the trainer the chance to understand and observe the moments in your routine that obedience could benefit.

Following this visit your trainer will formulate a customized training plan for you and your canines genre of life. Our goal is to establish a strong foundation built on trust and communication, tailored to your dog's unique needs and your realistic expectations. If all goes well, you'll then begin joining us for Band Practice!
