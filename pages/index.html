Welcome to Offbeat Canine, your trusted partner in dog training services based in Londonderry, NH.

I'm Megan, the driving force behind Offbeat Canine Training

Dedicated to bringing harmony and understanding to the relationship between dogs and their owners.

My journey into the world of dog training began over a decade ago when I embarked on my first job as a dog walker. It was during this time that I encountered a diverse array of canine companions, each with their own unique personalities and behaviors. From lively Vizslas to steadfast Dobermans, I found myself captivated by the intricacies of canine behavior and the deep bond between dogs and their humans.

My passion for understanding and connecting with dogs led me to explore various avenues within the canine industry, from daycare facilities to rescue organizations. It was at Doberman Rescue Unlimited where I received invaluable mentorship and guidance, further fueling my desire to pursue a career in dog training.

Driven by a passion for continuous learning and growth, I pursued formal education at CATCH Canine Academy, where I honed my skills through a comprehensive curriculum. Combining my hands-on experience with theoretical knowledge, I've developed a holistic approach to dog training that prioritizes education and understanding.

At Offbeat Canine Training, my mission is to revolutionize dog training by focusing on education. Just as humans invest in endless skill classes, I believe that dogs deserve the same level of education to thrive in their environments. I specialize in bridging the communication gap between dogs and their handlers, empowering humans to speak the language of their canine companions.

My training tools are designed to be user-friendly, accompanied by a documented timeline of our progress with your dog. I offer personalized in-home sessions, starting with a comprehensive consultation, followed by a package tailored to meet your specific needs. With standardized pricing for standardized education, I ensure that every client receives the highest quality training experience.

Join me on this journey to unlock the full potential of your dog and cultivate a harmonious bond based on mutual understanding and respect. Contact Offbeat Canine Training today to take the first step towards a brighter future with your furry friend!

Joining The Band

At Offbeat Canine, we're committed to making quality training accessible to all, because every dog deserves the chance to thrive. We offer simple packages with transparent pricing.

Audition

Following a free phone consultation, we'll schedule a visit to your home to observe your dog's behavior in their natural environment. During this session, we'll conduct a thorough behavioral evaluation and provide you with detailed insight on the behavior patterns. This visit gives the trainer the chance to understand and observe the moments in your routine that obedience could benefit.

Following this visit your trainer will formulate a customized training plan for you and your canines genre of life. Our goal is to establish a strong foundation built on trust and communication, tailored to your dog's unique needs and your realistic expectations. If all goes well, you'll then begin joining us for Band Practice!

One hour $65

Band Practice

Congrats, you're in the band! Join us for four or six weekly private training sessions. Mix and match between two types of practice: Garage Sessions and Trainer Tours! Either option includes the following:

  • A training plan customized to your dog's needs, updated every visit by our trainer.
  • Homework activities to help obedience become a natural part of your daily routine.
  • 24/7 email support from the trainer.
  • Customized training resources, made for humans to understand.
  • Videos made for reference with your trainer and your dog!
  • Access to Townie Tune Ups!

Garage Session 🥁

With your pups performance in mind, we offer Practice packages of four or six weeks of personalized private lessons conducted in the comfort of your own home. With our rock-inspired approach, expect a blend of passion, dedication, and plenty of positive reinforcement. Let's hit the stage together, creating harmony and rhythm between you and your beloved canine companion.
