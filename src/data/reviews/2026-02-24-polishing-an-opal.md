When we first reached out to Megan, owner and operator of Offbeat Canine Training, we were desperate for help with our puppy Opal. She was very different from our first dog Olive, as Opal prefers to ask for forgiveness rather than permission! She had so much energy and love to give, but didn't know how to control it, leaving my husband and I feeling completely overwhelmed.

Within just one session with Megan, we saw an immediate improvement in Opal. Megan introduced us to structured play and helped us implement the proper leash and collar for her, which made a huge difference right away. Over the past few months of working with Megan, our lives have truly changed.

Both our puppy Opal and our 5-year-old dog Olive have transformed. We can now have people over without being jumped on or barked at (thank you, place!). Walks are no longer stressful, we can walk without barking or pulling at people, animals, cars, etc. Megan has also helped build our confidence as dog owners, encouraging us every step of the way and providing clear, positive guidance and corrections when needed.

I can’t recommend Megan and Off Beat Canine Training enough! She truly takes the time to get to know you and your dog(s) and creates a customized plan for skill building. She equips you as a handler with the skills you need to keep making progress between sessions. She has truly changed our lives, and we can’t wait to continue working with her and growing our skills even more!
